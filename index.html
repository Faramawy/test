CWB is an intellectual property consultancy which provides IP services in 22 countries throughout the Middle East and North Africa. We counsel clients ranging from entrepreneurial start-ups to multinational corporations and assists them in implementing solutions to obtain, manage, protect, enforce, and commercialise their intellectual property.

Our teams manage, protect and enforce IP rights for our clients across a section of industries, including oil & gas, media, technology, transportation, entertainment, consumer electronics, tobacco, pharmaceuticals, confectionery, FMCG, property development, luxury fashion and cosmetics.
